Cacio e Pepe

Description

Who doesn't love cheese, pasta, and pepper? This dish is often referred to as 'adult mac and cheese', eliciting nostalgic memories of consuming copious portions of Kraft as a child-- but with a mature, upscale twist. Whether you're after a quick lunch or a romantic home-cooked dinner, cacio e pepe is the perfect comfort food for all occasions.

Ingredients

  • 200g bucatini or spaghetti noodles
  • 25g butter
  • 2tsp ground whole black peppercorns (or 1tsp freshly ground black pepper)
  • 50g pecorino or parmesan, finely grated

Steps

  1. Cook pasta 2 minutes shy of box directions in salted, boiling water. Meanwhile, melt butter in a medium pan over low heat, and toast black pepper for a few minutes.
  2. Drain pasta, saving 200ml of the pasta water. Add cooked pasta and half of pasta water into pan with butter and pepper. Toss briefly.
  3. Scatter over the parmesan evenly, but don't stir-- wait for cheese to melt for 30 seconds. Once melted, toss everything well and stir together, adding more pasta water as needed.
  4. Serve immediately with a fresh grating of black pepper.
