Stuffed Peppers

Description

An easy entry-point dish for any inexperienced chef, stuffed peppers are filling, health, and oh-so easy to make. This is truly a dish the whole family can enjoy-- including the picky eaters!

Ingredients

  • 4 large bell peppers (any color)
  • 1lb lean ground beef (at least 80% lean)
  • 2tbsp chopped onion
  • 1c cooked rice
  • 1tsp salt
  • 1 clove garlic, finely chopped
  • 1 can (15oz) tomato sauce
  • 3/4c (3oz) shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Carefully slice bell peppers, removing the stem & top of each pepper, and any seeds or membranes inside. Be careful to preserve integrity of bell pepper.
  3. In a medium skillet, cook beef and onion over medium heat for 8 to 10 minutes, stirring occassionally until beef is brown. Drain.
  4. Stir in rice, salt, garlic, and 1 cup of the tomato sauce. Cook until hot.
  5. Stuff peppers with beef mixture. Stand peppers upright in a baking dish, pouring remaining sauce over peppers.
  6. Cover dish tightly with foil and bake for 10 minutes. Uncover and bake another 15 minutes, or until peppers are tender.
  7. Sprinkle with cheese. Serve hot.
